SOUND ASSETS GUIDE
==================

To add sound effects to your Pokemon Guess Who game, add the following files to:
res/raw/

Recommended Sound Files:
1. card_flip.mp3 - Played when player flips/selects a card
2. eliminate.mp3 - Played when marking a card as eliminated  
3. win.mp3 - Played when game is won
4. game_over.mp3 - Played when game ends

You can find free Pokemon sound effects from:
- Freesound.org (https://freesound.org)
- Pokemon Fandom Wiki (https://pokemon.fandom.com/)
- Zapsplat (https://www.zapsplat.com)

Once you add the sound files:
1. Place them in app/src/main/res/raw/
2. The SoundManager class will automatically load and play them
3. Call the appropriate method:
   - soundManager.playCardFlip()
   - soundManager.playCardEliminate()
   - soundManager.playGameWin()
   - soundManager.playGameOver()

Format Requirements:
- Format: MP3 or WAV
- Sample Rate: 44100 Hz or 48000 Hz
- Bitrate: 128-320 kbps
- Duration: Keep under 3 seconds for quick feedback

Add to SoundManager usage in GameScreen:
    viewModel.togglePokemonElimination(pokemon)
    soundManager.playCardEliminate()  // Add this line
